About

Who Am I?

My name is Dan Soloha. I'm the first of five children, born to two Ukrainian immigrants. I am currently 21 and a student at Columbus State Community College, majoring in Game Development.

In my free time, I'm almost always playing a game (Battlefield 4 is my current favorite), spending time with my dog Jack, or working on personal projects.

As a child, I loved Lego. I could literally stay in my room all day just putting blocks together and pulling them apart, trying different combinations to create more and more complex, intricate builds. As I got older, that same creative energy made its way onto the computer - I could spend hours and hours playing different games and trying to push the limits in them. Eventually I started to try to figure out exactly how the games worked, and finally, began to try to create my own.

As I've said, I'm currently a student at CSCC, but I also have a full-time job as a tradesman (I'd say I work construction, but that's not entirely accurate). My father is a carpenter, and when I'm not working with him on whatever project he's currently wrapped up in, I'm helping my cousin with his projects (he has his own construction and remodeling company, though he's only a year older than me).

I'd love to create my own game. There's nothing that beats the feeling, that rush you get when someone compliments something that you've created, and that feeling is one that drives me - to create and innovate, to do what I've never done before, to learn something new.
